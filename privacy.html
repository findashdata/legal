Privacy Policy

Service: FindashData TikTok Automation
Last updated: 2026-02-05

This Privacy Policy explains how FindashData (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and stores information when you use our TikTok automation tools (“Service”).

1) Information we collect

Depending on what you connect and request, the Service may collect and store:

  • OAuth tokens (access token and, if provided, refresh token) used to authenticate to TikTok on your behalf
  • Account metadata returned by TikTok APIs (e.g., account identifiers and basic profile information if provided)
  • Analytics data you request (e.g., views, likes, comments, watch-time metrics where available)
  • Upload metadata (e.g., filenames, timestamps, status of uploads/posts)

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information unless you provide it.

2) How we use information

We use the information to:

  • authenticate API requests to TikTok
  • publish content and retrieve analytics you request
  • troubleshoot errors and improve reliability of the automation

3) Where data is stored

Tokens and logs are typically stored locally on the machine running the Service (your own computer/server). We do not sell your data.

4) Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share information only:

  • with TikTok, as necessary to operate the Service via their APIs
  • with any service providers strictly required to run the Service (if used)

5) Data retention

We keep tokens and logs only as long as necessary for the Service to function, or until you request deletion. You can also revoke access from TikTok’s app/account settings where available.

6) Your choices & controls

You can:

  • revoke the Service’s access from TikTok (where available)
  • request deletion of locally stored tokens/logs
  • stop using the Service at any time

7) Security

We take reasonable steps to protect stored credentials (e.g., access controls and minimizing exposure). No method of storage is 100% secure.
